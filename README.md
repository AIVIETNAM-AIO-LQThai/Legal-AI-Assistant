# SME Legal AI Assistant

An AI-powered legal assistant designed for Vietnamese small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).
The system helps users ask business-related legal questions in Vietnamese, retrieves relevant legal documents, and generates answers with citations, legal basis, risk awareness, and clear uncertainty handling.

This project was developed for the **Road to AI 2026 (R2AI)** competition under the theme **Build AI Legal Assistant**.

---

## 1. Project Overview

SMEs in Vietnam often face difficulties when searching for and understanding legal documents related to business operations, tax, labor, contracts, compliance, and administrative procedures.

This project aims to build a practical AI assistant that can:

* Understand Vietnamese legal questions from SME users.
* Retrieve relevant legal documents and legal articles.
* Generate clear and grounded answers.
* Provide citations to support each answer.
* Warn users when the question lacks enough information.
* Reduce hallucination by forcing answers to rely on retrieved legal sources.

The goal is not to replace lawyers, but to support users with an initial legal understanding and help them know what to check next.

---

## 2. Main Features

### Legal Question Answering

The assistant can answer common Vietnamese SME-related legal questions, such as:

* Business registration requirements
* Tax obligations
* Labor and employment rules
* Contract-related issues
* SME support policies
* Compliance and administrative procedures

### Retrieval-Augmented Generation

The system uses a retrieval-based approach to search for relevant legal documents before generating an answer.

Instead of relying only on the language model’s memory, the assistant first finds supporting legal sources, then generates an answer based on those sources.

### Citation Support

Each answer includes references to relevant legal documents and articles, allowing users to verify the legal basis.

### Hallucination Control

The assistant is designed to avoid unsupported claims.
When the available information is insufficient, the system should state that clearly instead of guessing.

### SME-Focused Legal Guidance

Answers are written in a practical and understandable way for business users, not only for legal experts.

## 3. Expected Answer Format

The assistant is designed to answer legal questions using a structured format:

```text
Short answer:
A direct and concise answer to the user’s question.

Legal basis:
The legal documents and articles used to support the answer.

Explanation:
A simple explanation of how the legal rules apply.

Missing facts:
Important information that is still needed to give a more accurate answer.

Risk level:
Low / Medium / High, depending on the legal uncertainty or business risk.

Next step:
Recommended action for the user.

Disclaimer:
This answer is for reference only and is not a substitute for professional legal advice.
```

## 4. System Architecture

The system follows a Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline:

```text
User Question
     ↓
Question Preprocessing
     ↓
Legal Document Retrieval
     ↓
Relevant Article Selection
     ↓
Answer Generation
     ↓
Citation Validation
     ↓
Final Response
```

### Main Components

* **User Interface**
  A simple web interface where users can enter legal questions and view answers.

* **Retriever**
  Searches the legal document database and returns relevant documents or articles.

* **Generator**
  Uses a language model to generate an answer based on retrieved legal content.

* **Validator**
  Checks whether the answer is supported by citations and whether unsupported claims exist.

* **Output Formatter**
  Converts the final answer into the required structured format.

## 6. Evaluation Focus

The project focuses on two main evaluation areas:

### 1. Legal Information Retrieval

The system should retrieve the correct legal documents and articles related to the user’s question.

Important metrics include:

* Precision
* Recall
* F2-score

### 2. Legal Question Answering

The generated answer should be evaluated based on:

* Correct legal basis
* Accuracy of content
* Completeness
* Clarity
* Practical usefulness
* Proper limitation and disclaimer

Clone the repository:

```bash
git clone https://github.com/your-username/sme-legal-ai-assistant.git
cd sme-legal-ai-assistant
```

Create a virtual environment:

```bash
python -m venv venv
```
